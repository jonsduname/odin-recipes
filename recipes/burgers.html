Burgers

Description

This burger recipe is easy if you follow all the steps. They keys to a great burger is a well seasoned patty, melted cheese, and a toasted bun. You can add whatever else you'd like, such as onions, mushrooms, sliced tomatoes, and pickles.

This burger recipe will focus on the key components

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Patties
  • cheese
  • salt
  • melted butter

Steps

  1. Spread the buns with melted butter and put on med high heat pan
  2. Season the patty with salt
  3. Remove the buns when it is lightly brown or slightly toasted
  4. Put the patty onto the hot pan
  5. Once you flip over the patty, add sliced cheese on top
  6. Assemble the burger with whatever condiments you'd like
